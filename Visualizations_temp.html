Max Temperature



As expected the weather becomes warmer as one approaches the equator (0 Deg latitude). More interstingly, however, is the fact that that the souther hemisphere tends to be warmer this time of year than the northern hemisphere. This may be due to the tilt of the earth during which the data was collected.
